The Lyrics

The lyrics of “You Found Me” tell a tale of love and vulnerability. It portrays the journey of two individuals who were lost in their own worlds until they found each other. The first verse illustrates the feeling of being lost in a world of thoughts, symbolized by a sea of stars. Despite the distance between them, the singer held onto hope, wishing for something worth trying. The line “Who knew that we’d fallen faster than the speed of light?” emphasizes the unexpected and intense nature of their connection.

The pre-chorus indicates a transformation occurring within the singer. They close their eyes and brace themselves, ready to become someone else. The lyrics suggest that this love has empowered them to embrace change and let go of their fears. The line “I’m so happy you found me” reflects the joy and gratitude they feel for their partner’s presence in their life.

In the chorus, the phrase “Pull me under, feel the thunder” denotes the intensity of their love. It signifies a desire to be fully immersed and consumed by the strength of their emotions. The lyrics convey a sense of bliss and contentment with the line “I’m so happy you found me,” highlighting the singer’s appreciation for their partner’s impact on their life.

The second verse continues to explore the overwhelming nature of their connection. The line “Feels like we’re breakin’ through the ceiling” suggests that their love transcends boundaries and limitations. Each kiss leaves the singer breathless, and they find themselves willingly fighting the tension that arises from their relationship.
