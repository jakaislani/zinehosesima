Vinnie Politan Family and Personal life:

Politan is son of federal judge Nicholas H. Politan and bother to a corporate attorney and was born in New Jersey, United States. He was captain of the basketball team in West Orange High School, from where he did his high school as well. He studied communication in Stanford University of California before attending Seton Hall University School of Law located at Newark, New Jersey. He was in relationship with Lisa Frey before he married to Dolly Politan in 2002. The couple has two children together.

Vinnie Politan Career Growth:

Before starting a career in Journalism, Politan was a Prosecutor working in Bergen County, New Jersey. He then moved on to work in private legal sector that includes Carella-Byrne law firm and a supermarket chain- Grand Union.

Politan worked as an anchor and reporter at Channel 10 News in New Jersey covering political debates, legal stories and as well as general news. He also was producer and host of legal program “Bergen County Justice with Vinnie Politan”. He also worked as AP reporter and an anchor for Central Florida News 13. He covered both National Conventions of Democratic and Republic in 2000 as a correspondent to Time Warner.  He anchored on “Prime News with Vinnie Politan” and HLN Special program on HLN. He then created and hosted “After Dark” on HLN. Cases covered by Politan included Trayvon Martin, Jodi Arias, Michael Skakel, Michael Jackson, Casey Anthony, the Sean Combs case, the Zacarias Moussaoui trial, Dr. Conrad Murray, the Kobe Bryant rape case, and the Scott Peterson murder trial. He also hosted “Me and Vinnie” on the morning radio show- Sirius XM radio’s “Stars Too station”.He left HLN on 2014 and has joined a morning broadcast WXIA:”11 Alive”, a NBC affiliate as main anchor in the morning weekday news.

Aside from journalism, he is an A&R Director at Metropolitan Recording Corporation,which he co-founded in 1989. He is also co-founder of Ultimate Sports Association. He had his share of acting life too: he appeared in Twelve Angry Man, a Broadway production and as a detective in a TV series- “Law and Order”.
