Chicago-Style Hot Dogs and Our Labor Day Party Plans

Chicago-Style Hot Dogs are the perfect way to enjoy your hot dogs year-round!

Course Entree Cuisine American Keyword chicago style hot dogs Prep Time 10 minutes minutes Cook Time 5 minutes minutes Total Time 15 minutes minutes Servings 6 Calories 314kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

Ingredients

  • 6 all-beef hot dog
  • 6 hot dog buns
  • 6 tbsp yellow mustard
  • 6 tbsp sweet green pickle relish
  • 6 tbsp chopped onion
  • 2 tomatoes cut into wedges
  • 6 dill pickle spears
  • 12 pepperoncini peppers
  • celery salt to taste

Instructions

  • Grill hot dogs over medium heat until heated through.

  • Spread mustard and relish on hot dog bun.

  • Place hot dog in bun and top with remaining toppings.

  • Serve immediately and enjoy!
