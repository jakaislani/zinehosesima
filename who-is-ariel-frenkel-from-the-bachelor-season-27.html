Who Is Ariel Frenkel From The Bachelor Season 27?

Publish date: 2024-09-01

In the forthcoming season of The Bachelor, 30 single women will compete to be the one Zach falls in love with. Ariel Frenkel, a self-described adrenaline seeker from New York City, is one of the ladies vying for the final rose. The 28-year-old marketing expert hails from a huge Ukrainian family and wishes to discover love similar to that of her parents. Aerial describes herself as “adventurous, intellectual, and unashamedly”.

Meet Ariel Frenkel before she appears on The Bachelor.

The Bachelor season 27 cast member enjoys traveling and learning. The 28-year-old marketing professional has traveled extensively and learned new skills virtually wherever she goes. While Frenkel is from New York, she moved to Barcelona in 2010 to study creative writing and art history as part of the Oxbridge Academic Programs. She traveled to Italy two years later to attend the Florence University of Skills, where she did a culinary summer course in cooking and associated culinary arts. During her study, she learned how to create Italian food by area, as well as the history of each.

She then attended George Washington University, where she received a bachelor’s degree in arts and organizational sciences. If her internships are included, The Bachelor cast member has nearly a decade of experience working in many areas, including public relations and marketing.

While the season 27 cast member is now a freelance marketing consultant, she previously worked as a campus representative for Tinder and as an intern at the Cannes Film Festival. Frenkel assisted Lionsgate and Summer Entertainment with tickets, client connections, and worldwide distribution of events and initiatives at Cannes.

In 2014, she worked as a theater PR intern for Lionsgate. Her responsibilities included performing media research for their Los Angeles and New York offices, as well as producing reports on new film releases and other administrative tasks. The Bachelor season 27 participant is quite active on social media, with 6,896 Instagram followers. She often posts images of her trips on social media, and she has visited Italy, France, Spain, the United Kingdom, Mexico, Iceland, Hong Kong, and other places. Frenkel announced her attendance in the program on Instagram on January 5.

The caption stated:

“@Jessepalmer referred to me as a thrill seeker, therefore here I am.”

Frenkel wants to meet someone who can match her enthusiasm while on The Bachelor. When she isn’t traveling the globe, she enjoys exploring her city by taking long walks and listening to SZA, according to her ABC profile. While nothing is certain, Reality Steve, a renowned blogger, has revealed some details about the forthcoming season.

Frenkel makes it to the top four in the future season in photographs shared on the blogger’s Twitter on November 3, 2022, as Zach is seen on a hometown date with her as they stroll through Washington Square Park. Watch what happens as The Bachelor season 27 begins on ABC on January 23 at 8:00 p.m. ET.
