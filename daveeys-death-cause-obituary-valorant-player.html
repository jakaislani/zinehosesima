Daveeys death cause has been linked to decompensation. Find out more about the Valorant player’s obituary in the today’s article.

Santiago Ruiz, famous as Daveeys, was a prominent Colombian player who last played for KRÜ Esports. He was widely noted for being a valorant player.

Furthermore, Daveeys was a former professional Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player who was known for his time in Infinity Esports. 

His career kicked off with LAZER, and he quickly rose up the ranks of the Latin American North region as one of the best Duelist players.

Likewise, he was the star of the E-Xolos LAZER roster that would defeat the Latin America North region for the entirety of 2022. 

Daveeys Death Cause Linked To Decompensation

Daveeys death cause has been linked to decompensation.

KRÜ Esports confirmed the sad news on September 4, 2023, saying that the Valorant player took his last breath on September 3.

Not to mention, his 23rd birthday was on September 2. With the tragic announcement, people were shocked and started sharing their words for the late player.

Many sources have said that Daveeys died after suffering decompensation.

For your information, Decompensation is a loss of physiological compensation or psychological balance, especially the inability of the heart to keep adequate circulation.

Daveeys Obituary and Funeral Details

Daveeys obituary was shared following his death on September 3, 2023. At the time of his passing, the Valorant player was just 23 years old. KRÜ Esports shared the news on social media.
