Who is Khushi Punjaban? Wiki, Bio, Age, Husband &amp; Facts About YouTuber

Publish date: 2024-09-11

Khushi Punjaban (born on December 09, 1995) is a well-known TikToker, YouTube star, Model, actress, entrepreneur, and social media influencer from India. She rose to fame for her lip-sync and short videos with her husband Vivek Choudhary on TikTok. The couple also owns a self-titled YouTube channel.

Moreover, she has millions of fans on all her social media accounts. She has also appeared in many music videos of the Punjabi language. She is becoming famous in the country after her marriage. The bond between the Khushi and Vivek is liked by the people. 

Quick Facts / Wiki

Biography

Khushi took birth on 09 December 1995 in New Delhi, India. She is living in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India with her husband. Her full name is Khushi Choudhary. 

She has completed her education in New Delhi. She went to The Delhi University for her Bachelor of Business Administration studies. She is fond of acting and modeling since childhood. 

Family & Caste

Khushi Punjaban mostly uploads pictures and videos with her biological parents but she has not shared their names and profession. She loves her father and mother and they also complete all her wishes before even being told. 

We have no information about the siblings of Khushi. However, she follows the Hindu religion and as per Wikipedia, she holds Rajput Brahmin descent.

Personal life

Khushi Punjaban is a beautiful model and actress, who got married in 2017 to Vivek Choudhary. Vivek has won the title of ‘Mr. Uttrakhand’ in 2018. They are enjoying their lives with each other. They featured in many music videos together.

Husband & Boyfriend

In August 2020, Khushi gave birth to a child, a baby boy, who was named ‘Raja Choudhary’. They named him Raja because Vivek’s brother had died before, whose name was Raja. Khushi and Vivek made an account on Instagram of their son and write ‘Raja Is Back’ in bio. 

Family-in-law

Khushi Choudhary loves her in-laws a lot and also made videos with them. She uploads pictures with her mother-in-law named Anita Choudhary. She also made videos with her Father-in-law named Mahikar Singh and Nishu Choudhary (sister-in-law).

Career

Khushi started her career with modeling as she has a keen interest in fashion and ramp walks. Later, she started making videos on a popular app ‘TikTok’. People got attracted to the chemistry of Khushi and her husband. The couple starts becoming popular in the country. Due to the war between India and China, all China’s apps got banned in the nation, which results in a ban on TikTok. At that time, she has 16 million fans. Apart from this, they use to make videos on YouTube. Afterward, they start making videos on Instagram reels and MX Takatak.

YouTube

Khushi Punjaban has made an account on YouTube on September 12, 2013, and as of April 2021, she has 428 million views on her channel under the name ‘Mr & Mrs. Choudhary’. Khushi made videos of humor, comedy, daily vlogging, trips, and many more with her husband Vivek Choudhary. The most viewed video of her channel is titled ‘RAJA IS BACK | Choudhary Family Completed ❤️ | Khushi Punjaban | Vivek Choudhary’ which has more than 10 million views. The couple also made videos with their son Raja.

Music Videos

Khushi Punjaban has also featured in many songs of the Punjabi industry with Vivek Choudhary. She has worked in the song ‘Kangna’ by Sirat Maan along with her husband. She also appeared in Mann Jaa Ve by Kay Vee Singh, Mera Haq by Sahil Kanda, Hide Karke by Lakhi Natt, and Sohni Lagdi by Rohanpreet Singh. From her Instagram account, we came to know that she is working on her upcoming song named ‘Aunde Rahe’ by Param Sabi. 

Social Media Influencer

Khushi has millions of fan followers on all her social media accounts. On Instagram, she has 3.3 million followers till April 2021. She often uploads her gorgeous pictures and stunning videos with her family. She made regular reels that gather thousands of views every time.

Net Worth

Khushi is earning well from all her income sources. She gets INR 40-50k (approx.) from her music videos and earns in thousands from her videos on YouTube and other social media account. A rough guess of her net worth is around INR 5-6 Crores approximately, which includes the income from her collaborations and commercials too.
