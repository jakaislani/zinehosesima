Anuel AA wife: Who is Yailin la Mas Viral?

Publish date: 2024-07-15

By the stage moniker Anuel AA, Emmanuel Gazmey Santiago is better recognized in the music industry. He is a well-known rapper and vocalist who was born in Puerto Rico. He is also credited for helping to create Latin trap music.

The artist has achieved enormous success in his life thanks to his remarkable skill. He frequently interpolates or samples well-known tunes from his youth into his music.

In July 2018, he issued his debut album Real Hasta la Muerte. He has since put out a number of popular songs and albums, including La Llevo Al Cielo, Adicto, Mercedes Tintia, Delincuente, and many others.
