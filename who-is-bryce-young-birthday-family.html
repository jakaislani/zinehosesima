Who Is Bryce Young?, Birthday, Family, Social Media, Net Worth: Born on July 25, 2001, Bryce Young, officially known as Bryce Christopher Young is an American football player.

He developed a love for playing American football at a younger age and has been consistent throughout his career rising through the ranks to become one of the most sought-after American football players.

At the time of this writing (April 2023), Bryce Young is an American football quarterback for the Carolina Panthers of the National Football League.

He attended Cathedral High School in Los Angeles and transferred to Mater Dei High School in Santa Ana, California, for his last two years of high school football.

For his University education, Bryce Young after initially committing to the University of Southern California (USC) to play football, de-committed and decided to play at the University of Alabama for Nick Saban.

He holds the record for most passing yards in a single game by an Alabama quarterback and was the recipient of the 2021 Heisman Trophy.

Bryce won the title of the USA Today Offensive Player of the Year in 2018 and in 2019, he won the U.S. Army Player of the Year Award in 2019.

In 2021, he won several notable awards which include; the 2021 Rose Bowl, 2021 CFP National Championship, 2021 Cotton Bowl Classic, the Heisman Trophy 2021 as well as the Maxwell Award 2021.

Overall, Young passed for 4,872 yards, 47 passing touchdowns, and seven interceptions to go along with three rushing touchdowns in 15 games.

In April 2023, Bryce Young hit major headlines when he was selected by the Carolina Panthers as the first overall pick in the 2023 NFL Draft on Thursday, April 27.
