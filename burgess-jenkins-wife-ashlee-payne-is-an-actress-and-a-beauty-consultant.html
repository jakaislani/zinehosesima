Burgess Jenkins Wife Ashlee Payne Is An Actress And A Beauty Consultant

Publish date: 2024-07-10

American actor Burgess Jenkins wife, Ashlee Payne, is a professional actress and beauty consultant. The couple has been married for more than a decade. 

Jenkins is renowned for his work in Remember the Titans, opposite Denzel Washington. He was also Hilary Swank’s onscreen husband, David Winter, in the 2007 film The Reaping. 

The 49-year-old has been active in the entertainment and movie industry since 2000. Speaking of his career, the actor portrayed John Wesley in the award-winning film Wesley with Kevin McCarthy and June Lockhart. 

Likewise, Jenkins also appeared as Bobby Irons throughout season six of the hit CW series One Tree Hill. Additionally, he has appeared as a guest star on Drop Dead Diva, Revolution, and JAG. In the real story Unshackled, Jenkins co-starred alongside Stacy Keach. 

Burgess was nominated in the Best Actor category at the Boston Film Festival for playing the role of Bruce Snow in Insignificant Others.

Meet Burgess Jenkins Wife Ashlee Payne

Burgess Jenkins is happily married to his wife Ashlee Payne, a cancer survivor.

Payne is available on the social media platform Instagram under the username @farmasiafter40. Her bio suggests that she is a makeup enthusiast, a momma, and a breast cancer survivor. 

Ashlee Is An Actress

Ashlee Is A Beauty Consultant

She primarily works in helping people with mature skin. At the same time, the actress also ensures that her client finds makeup and skincare that suits them the best.
