Donna Arruda obituary passed away at the age of 68

Publish date: 2024-07-08

Donna (Trahan) Arruda, aged 68, hailing from Seekonk and formerly of Dighton, unexpectedly passed away on Friday, December 29, 2023. Her departure followed a tragic accident involving a drunk driver. Donna’s 38-year-old husband, Floriano Arruda, and their grandson, Jacoby Arruda, whom they had lovingly raised, also met a devastating end in the same accident.

She is survived by her children, Lisa Rebello Napert (husband Brock), Carl Rebello, Lisa Arruda-Graham (husband John), Keith Arruda, and the late Ryan Arruda. Siblings include Richard Trahan (late wife Connie), Robert Trahan, Scott Trahan, Steven Trahan, Stacey (Trahan) DeCosta (husband Derek), and Sidney Trahan, Jr. Donna is also remembered by her late granddaughters and grandsons, Dawn Trahan, Alison Trahan, Matthew Mendes, Amber Allan, Ashlyn Ferreira, Autumn Trahan, William Trahan, Heather Trahan-Martins, Christopher Trahan, and Jason Trahan.

Her grandchildren, Alexis Pacheco, Aliyah Fonseca, Kory Arruda, Robert John (RJ) Graham, Jordan Dion, Makayla Pimentel, Austin Arruda, Hailey Arruda, Keith Arruda Jr., and Alexis Arruda, as well as great-grandchildren Eden Mae Melo, Paityn Dion-Zahara, Kynslee Pimentel, and numerous other relatives and friends, will deeply miss her.

Born in Fall River, MA, Donna was the daughter of the late Sidney and Irene (Benoit) Trahan. Donna dedicated her career to the Dighton Rehoboth School System. She found beauty in everything, always with a cup of coffee in hand, generously giving to her loved ones and radiating infectious laughter. Donna was a resilient and strong woman, fighting until the end. Our only solace is knowing she is reunited in heaven with her beloved Jacoby and Flo, and at peace alongside her son Ryan.

Her visitation will be held on Thursday, January 4, from 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM and from 4:00 PM to 8:00 PM at Auclair Funeral Home, 690 South Main St., Fall River. The funeral Mass will take place on Friday, January 5, at 10:00 AM at St. John of God Church, Brayton Ave., Somerset.

In the wake of Donna Arruda’s death, the grief is palpable, and her memory will forever be etched in the hearts of those who knew and loved her. The Arruda family expresses their gratitude for the outpouring of support during this challenging time.

May Donna Arruda and her departed loved ones rest in peace.
