Where to buy 'hide yo kids hide yo wife' star Antoine Dodson's beer

Publish date: 2024-06-29

ANTOINE Dodson rose to fame in 2010 after a video of him saying "hide yo kids, hide yo wife" went viral.

Now, he is returning to the spotlight with the release of his new beer, "Run N Tell That."

Where can I buy Antoine Dodson's beer?

During Dodson's interview back in 2010, he ended it by telling an alleged rapist to "run and tell that," which later became the name of his beer.

According to TMZ, the beer will be released on September 24, 2022, at a "huge party," but at this time, it is unclear if the beer will make its way to stores.

TMZ notes that the beer is being brewed in his hometown of Huntsville, Alabama, and the partnership was set up by one of his friends.

Who is Antoine Dodson?

Antoine Dodson is a popular internet celebrity, singer, and actor who was born on June 27, 1986.

In 2010, he gave an interview to a local news station after his sister was attacked in their home.

In the passionate interview, Dodson said "hide yo kids, hide yo wife" which quickly became a viral meme.

The phrase was also remixed into the Bed Intruder Song by The Gregory Brothers, which eventually went Platinum.

Dodson later cashed in on his newfound fame by dabbling in acting and went on to appear alongside Carson Daly at NBC's 2011 New Years' special.

He also joined Tyler Perry's A Madea Christmas in 2013.

Now, Dodson lives a quieter life as a father and substitute teacher, according to Paper Magazine.

What is Antoine Dodson's net worth?

While Dodson's internet fame might have faded away, he was still able to cash in on his 15 minutes of fame.

As of August 2022, he has an estimated net worth of $50,000, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

This estimate comes from the success of his career as well as other business ventures.
