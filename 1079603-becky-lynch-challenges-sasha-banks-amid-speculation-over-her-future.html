Becky Lynch Challenges Sasha Banks Amid Speculation Over Her Future

Publish date: 2024-09-02

RAW & SmackDown Women’s Champion Becky Lynch has never been one to shy away from a fight, and she’s certainly never been one to hide away from a hot topic on social media. It’s no secret that Sasha Banks‘ future is up in the air, with recent reports suggesting that she tried to quit WWE in the wake of her WrestleMania 35 loss. Now, in a move that’ll continue to add fuel to the fire, Lynch has decided to challenge her Four Horsewomen teammate to a fight.
